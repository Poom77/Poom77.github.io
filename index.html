Projets

1. Zero-dimensional Modeling of the cardiovascular system in Fontan patients:

Supervisors: Dr. Beatrice Riviere and Dr. Charles Puelz. Done at Rice University.

The Fontan procedure is a common surgery for patients with born with a single ventricular heart. The surgery eventually entails the reconstruction and connection of the aorta to the single ventricle and connecting the vena cavae to the pulmonary arteries. Building upon previous works on zero dimensional Fontan models, our project sought to model how the Fontan circuit is affected by exercise as well as adopting efficient numerical algorithms to solve such models.
