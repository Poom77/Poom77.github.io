About Me

My name is Poom Kritpracha. I recently graduated from Rice University with a double major in Computational and Applied Mathematics (CAAM) and Mathematics (MATH). I will be attending CU Boulder this upcoming fall semester for graduate studies in Applied Mathematics (APPM). I am currently working on the implementation of a zero-dimensional solute transport model for the circulatory system under the supervision of Dr. Beatrice Riviere and Dr. Charles Puelz. I have a general interest in dynamical systems, numerical linear algebra, and their applications to questions in biology and physiology. I am also interested in social history and social theory.
